KONEKITA: SANTOLAN LABOR HUB

KoneKita: Santolan Labor Hub Web Portal An innovative local online platform designed to connect skilled workers, freelancers, and professionals from Santolan to potential clients, employers, and business opportunities. KoneKita serves as a centralized hub for job matching, service promotion, and community networking. By enabling direct market access and reducing the gap between labor and demand, the platform empowers local talents, promotes inclusive employment, and contributes to the sustainable economic growth of the Santolan community. KoneKita also supports digital literacy and modern work readiness by providing an accessible space for showcasing skills, building client relationships, and finding income-generating opportunities—all in one convenient online portal.
