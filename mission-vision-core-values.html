Mission

To empower the youth of Barangay Santolan by fostering their potential, promoting active participation in community development, and ensuring transparent, responsive, and youth-centered governance.

Vision

A progressive, inclusive, and empowered youth community that leads change, supports development, and inspires future generations through dedicated service and innovation.

Core Values

Leadership

We lead by example and inspire others through integrity and initiative.

Service

Our actions are rooted in the spirit of service to our community and fellow youth.

Transparency

We uphold honesty and openness in all our endeavors and governance.

Inclusivity

We value and respect the diversity and voice of every Santoleno youth.

Innovation

We embrace creativity and new ideas to create positive impact and change.
