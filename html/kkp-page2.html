INFORMED CONSENT AND DATA PRIVACY STATEMENT

INFORMED CONSENT

in accordance to DILG Memorandum Circular 2022-033, series of 2022


I. Title of Research

Katipunan ng Kabataan Profiling (KK Profiling)

II. Consulting Agency

National Youth Commission (NYC), Quezon City, Philippines

III. Purpose of the Study

The Profiling aims to gather the information and data of the Katipunan ng Kabataan members. The information that will be gathered in the KK Profiling will be stored to the upcoming SK Portal and will only be used for the purpose of database management handled by the National Youth Commission.

IV. Terms and Duration of Participation

You are asked to join the study as a participant in the KK Profiling. The conduct of the profiling will be done online via Google Forms; the data will serve as an updated National database of the Katipunan ng Kabataan members in the Philippines, and of the Sangguniang Kabataan ng Barangay Santolan, Pasig City.

V. Risks/Confidentiality

Your participation in the study will be treated with utmost confidentiality. Any information collected from you will be used in the Database management. Also, your safety is our primary concern. The profiling ensures that there will be no risk to encounter during the process of data collection, in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

VI. Compensation

The activity is in accordance with RA No. 10742 and the policy/guidelines of the Department of Interior and Local Government and the National Youth Commission. There will be no monetary remuneration other than our sincerest gratitude for your time and effort. Your participation will be highly appreciated.

VII. Inquiries

If you have any question/s on the administration of the survey question or the study in general, please do not hesitate to contact the research proponent through the following information:
